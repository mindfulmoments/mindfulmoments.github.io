Frequently Asked Questions

What insurances do you take?
Medicare, Blue Cross and Blue Shield (Companion Benefits Inc. and APS), CHP, Medicaide, and TriCare.

Will you tell anyone that I see you?
No. See the form Limits of Confidentiality for more information about times when therapists are required to release information.

Where do I park?
There are several parking spaces behind the house and there is on street parking.

Does the other therapist in your office see my records or do you talk about your cases with each other?
No. See the form Limits of Confidentiality for more detail and exceptions to confidentiality.

Which doctors or psychiatrists would you recommend I see for medication?
Referrals are based on diagnosis and treatment decisions and will be discussed in therapy.

Do you recommend medication or prescribe it?
Eunice is not a medical doctors and does not prescribe medication. For some mental illnesses there are effective medical treatments involving medication. Recommendations to see a doctor will be discussed in therapy. Clients are always encouraged to discuss their mental health issues with their doctor as well.

How long will I have to wait to get an appointment?
Appointments are usually available within the same month. However, Eunice does geriatric care management only part-time. So if you require a very specific time or day, it may take longer to get an appointment.
