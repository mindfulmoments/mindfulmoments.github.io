Group Therapy

"Be patient toward all that is unsolved in your heart and try to love the questions themselves, like locked rooms and like books that are now written in a very foreign tongue. Do not now seek the answers, which cannot be given you because you would not be able to live them. And the point is, to live everything. Live the questions now. Perhaps you will then gradually, without noticing it, live along some distant day into the answer. "
Rainer Maria Rilke

There are some problems that are better solved through the use of group therapy instead of individual therapy. The therapist will discuss this option with you if it is the recommended treatment. Some conditions for which group therapy is often more effective than individual therapy include: social anxiety, anxiety, relational problems, trust issues, eating disorders, and body image issues.

New Groups start October 2013: on Thursday mornings and Friday afternoons

Call 643-8449 to set up an interview with the leader to learn about the group, discuss insurance coverage, and decide your goals for the group.

Support groups including parenting, ADHD, looking for work, and caregivers are also possibile if we have six interested participants. If you would be interested in group therapy on this or another topic please email Eunice at elehmacher@gmail.com.
