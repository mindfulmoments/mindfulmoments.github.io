Sign up for a Mindfulness Class

Mindfulness is a skill that anyone can learn. The exercises are simple and with practice you can learn to ruminate less and enjoy each moment more. Each class will include practicing at least four mindfulness exercises, teaching segments, and learning from each other. Practices learned include: deep breathing, mindful walking, decreasing judgments, observing and letting go of thoughts, mindful eating, and many more. Participating with a group regularly will help you to continue to practice your skills. Mindfulness training can help anyone be more alert and aware of themselves and the world around them. The practices can be especially helpful in the treatment of: trouble sleeping, worry, anxiety, depression, physical pain, eating disorders, substance abuse disorders, and problems paying attention.
