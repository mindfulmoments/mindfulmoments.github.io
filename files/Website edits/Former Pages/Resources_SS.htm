Soiling Solutions


Soiling Solutions is a very effective treatment for encopresis (problems with soiling) and enuresis (problems with wetting). To find a description of the method or to purchase a treatment manual go to: www.soilingsolutions.com. Treatments are effective for children at least 3 years old.

Telephone consultation by Eunice Lehmacher is available to parents who have already purchased the Soiling Solutions manual and plan to or have already started to implement the Soiling Solutions protocol.

Many parents have been effective with Soiling Solutions by simply reading and applying the manual. However, no manual can anticipate all the concerns parents might have or the individuality of each child. Additional consultation with a therapist can help to answer questions, personalize the treatment, and help children who aren't making progress quickly enough.
