What is Mindfulness?

  • Keeping one's consciousness alive to the present reality. Thich Nhat Hanh
  • Being able to balance emotions and thoughts to become more aware of experiences of the outside world and the inner self.
  • The awareness that emerges through paying attention on purpose, in the present moment, and nonjudgmentally to the unfolding of experience moment to moment. Jon Kabat-Zinn
  • Developing a lifestyle of participating with awareness.
  • Opposite of mindlessness or multi-tasking.
  • Learning to control your mind without letting your mind control you.
  • Awareness of the present experience with acceptance. Christopher Germer
  • A simple skill that anyone can learn that gets easier to do and more effective with practice, practice, practice...
