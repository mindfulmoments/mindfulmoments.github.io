Spiritually Alive!
Learning to live more fully and with meaning


This seminar will help you to learn to find renewal from within. Through simple spiritual practices such as gratitude, acceptance, and awareness you will learn to find joy no matter what your circumstances and peace that follows you through your day. Although many spiritual practices or disciplines will be taught, you will choose those that fit you and your faith to apply to your life. Through the use of 12 simple words (e.g. here, oh, thanks, no), you'll learn spiritual practices that will draw you closer to God and help you feel more alive and joyful no matter what your circumstances.

The class is based on the book Naked Spirituality by Brian McLaren. You'll be asked to read a chapter from the book each week. The book is available for purchase here.

Each session is 50 minutes and includes a time of teaching, a time of quiet, reading from The Bible, practicing, learning from each other, and assignments. 12 sessions covering the spring (hopeful), summers (growing), winters (dark times), and autumns (renewal) of the spiritual life. A free 45 min. overview is available as a seminar format to churches in Seneca and Clemson. Costs of the 12 week class will depend on the size of the group (based on a fee of $50/hour for the leader). Material can also be covered in a retreat setting or in four 90 minute sessions with less detail.
